Chocolate Chip Cookies

Delicious chocolate chip cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 bag of chocolate chips
  • 1 tsp love

Steps

  1. Add sugar, flour, milk to a bowl
  2. Stir
  3. Add chocolate chips
  4. Pour into pan
  5. Heat oven to 10000c
  6. Put pan in oven
  7. Bake for 12 minutes
  8. Enjoy!
