Peanut Butter Stew

Creamy peanut butter stew

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of peanut butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 8 potatoes
  • 2 whole chickens
  • 1 tsp love

Steps

  1. Add peanut butter to bowl of milk
  2. Bring to a boil
  3. Add potatoes, chickens
  4. Boil for 30 minutes
  5. Serve and enjoy
